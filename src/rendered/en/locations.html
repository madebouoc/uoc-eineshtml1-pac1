La Sagrada Familia

Marvel at the monumental and iconic Sagrada Familia, the pinnacle and eternal legacy of brilliant architect Antoni Gaudí.

Park Güell

Immerse yourself in the magical Park Güell, Gaudí's masterpiece combining art, nature, and architecture in a unique space.

La Pedrera - Casa Milà

Admire the innovative Casa Milà, known as La Pedrera, a groundbreaking and iconic building by Gaudí in Barcelona.

Casa Batlló

Discover the captivating Casa Batlló, a dazzling example of Gaudí's creative genius and his ability to reinvent space.

Palau Güell

Visit the impressive Güell Palace, a modernist gem blending Gothic, Mudejar elements, and Gaudí's creative genius.

Casa Vicens

Behold Gaudí's first masterpiece, the colorful and unique Casa Vicens, where the architect showcases his creative talent.
